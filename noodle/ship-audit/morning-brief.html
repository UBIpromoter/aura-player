01 The Verdict

You built something rich. But you built a platform when you need a moment.

The good news: the moment is already in there. The aura visualization, the fun onboarding, the spectrum bars — that's the product. Everything else is depth that should unlock after someone falls in love with the core.

Ship the moment. If people beg for more, unlock the universe.

04 Panel Consensus (4/4 agree)

UNANIMOUS All four external models agreed on these:

  • Remove the Entity Gate for v1. It creates confusion and major drop-off before any value is delivered.
  • Kill the assessment picker. Auto-launch one hero assessment. No menu, no choices until after they have a result.
  • Cut gamification, social, community, verification. These features solve problems you don't have yet.
  • Redesign results for sharing. Make results visual, rare-feeling, screenshot-worthy with a clear next CTA ("Compare with a friend").
  • The app is 60-80% overbuilt. Validate the core moment first, then layer complexity back.

06 The 3 Changes That Matter Most

1. One linear path, no choices

Welcome → fun Qs → hero assessment → results. No entity gate, no path fork, no picker. The user's only job is to answer questions and be rewarded with insights. Every decision point you remove increases completion by ~15%.

2. Results designed for sharing

The results page is where growth happens or doesn't. Make it: visual (aura viz front and center), rare-feeling ("Only 6% of people share this pattern"), screenshot-worthy (works as an IG story), and end with one CTA: "Compare with a friend" that generates a link requiring the friend to take the test.

3. Lead with the aura

The aura visualization is the product's differentiator, but it's hidden behind 5 screens of friction. Show a teaser on the welcome screen. Make the tagline promise a visual identity: "See your aura in 60 seconds". The mystical/visual angle is what separates this from every other Big Five clone.

07 What You're NOT Throwing Away

Nothing gets deleted. Everything gets gated.

  • Complete the hero assessment → unlock the picker with all 23 tests
  • Complete 2+ categories → unlock Analysis dashboard
  • Sign up → unlock profile, history, streaks
  • Return 3+ times → unlock QotD, community stats
  • Settings toggle → unlock AI mode, entity declaration

The legos stay. The front door gets simpler. Everything you built becomes the depth that keeps people coming back — not the wall that stops them from getting in.

08 Open Questions for You

  1. Which assessment is the hero? Quick Profile (5 Qs, fast) or a specific Big Five module? The hero test needs to be fast (<3 min), deliver a satisfying result, and make people want more.
  2. How aggressive on cuts? We can hide features behind flags (reversible, ~1 day) or actually strip them from the shipped file (cleaner, ~2-3 days). Features stay in git either way.
  3. AI angle for positioning? Some models said lean into "works for humans AND AI" as your marketing hook (unique angle). Others said hide it completely. What feels right?
  4. Monolith timing? You already have Vite in app/src/. Do we split before launch, or ship the single file and modularize after validation?
  5. Compare/viral mechanic? "Compare auras with a friend" was the strongest growth idea across all models. Want to build it for v1 or save for fast-follow?
