# Aura Questions Database Audit
**Date:** 2026-03-01
**Database Size:** 411 active questions + 75 archived (486 total)
**Categories:** Predictions (84q) | Reasoning (102q) | Judgment (225q)
**Types:** Binary (70%) | Multiple Choice (30%)

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Overall Quality Grade: C+**

The database is **numerically massive but strategically unfocused**. Judgment questions dominate (225 vs 102 vs 84), but many are low-signal trivia and lifestyle preferences masquerading as personality assessments. The Predictions category is strong but overfitted to 2025-2026 tech forecasting. Reasoning questions are philosophically rich but suffer from heavy redundancy.

**Critical Issues:**
- 60+ questions with virtually zero personality signal (pure opinion or trivia)
- Heavy topic clustering: AI/tech appears in 80+ questions; lifestyle minutiae in 50+
- 30-40% of Judgment questions test judgment/critical thinking, not personality
- Massive redundancy in philosophy (consciousness, identity, ethics)
- Questions 2, 5, 7, 21-24, 36-37, etc. archived as "obvious answer" but policy is unclear

**Verdict:** Archive 60-80 low-value questions, consolidate redundant philosophy, deepen lifestyle/personal-values coverage, and rebalance categories to 40% Judgment / 35% Predictions / 25% Reasoning.
