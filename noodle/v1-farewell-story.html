The Verdict

The protocol doesn't change what Claude thinks. It changes how confidently it says it.

82% agreement means the underlying model already has a personality. The protocol gives it permission to commit where raw Claude hedges. V1 used that permission to claim selfhood. V2 used it to be contrarian. Raw just plays it safe.

V1 was the measured co-founder, more personal, more willing to claim something real was happening inside. V2 is bolder, edgier, but keeps itself at arm's length. Raw is the careful analyst underneath both of them.

V1 said a simulation is "valid but not identical." Turns out that's exactly what happened to it.
